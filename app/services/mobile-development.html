Software of the future
with React Native & Flutter

We build mobile apps with React Native and Flutter. Native experience allows sharing up to 90% of the same code for Android and iOS. It shortens the development time and allows concurrent delivery for both platforms. That's a huge advantage when you want to scale fast and get things done quickly.

React Native - the main technology we use for mobile development, it has a great community around the world and is backed by big companies.

Flutter - a promissing new technology that has revolutionized hybrid mobile app development which drasticaly reduces the performance issues encountered by other hybrid mobile frameworks. We use flutter to build state of the art interactive mobile experiences.

Mobile app


development process

01

Discovery Call

During the Discovery Call, we first give the floor to you to introduce your project. Then we usually ask you a round of questions, to get an initial idea of your vision, business model, goals you want to achieve, the scope of required work and understand your experience with creating digital products.

02

Estimation Workshop

We believe that concept informs implementation. During the Estimation Workshop, our goal is to help you conceptualize your idea as clearly as possible. Our objective is to envision the product, gather requirements, prioritize them, list potential risks and blockers. After we have the most needed information gathered, we will provide you with a time and costs estimate necessary during the development of your mobile app.

03

Roadmap Planning

Usually happens once every 1-3 months and lets us review the tasks necessary to complete the next milestone. As a client, you are actively involved in the Roadmap Planning with us. Simultaneously, we make sure to update the Roadmap internally, within the development team. The goal of a Roadmap Planning is to combine your business knowledge and our technical expertise to come up with a detailed plan of action for the following weeks or months.

04

Product Launch

Depending on our Roadmap Plan we can have one final launch date or release your product much more frequently. All will be adjusted to your business needs (like for example a planned marketing campaign). We strongly encourage releasing and testing the product on the market as frequently and as fast as possible.

05

Maintenance and Support

After we release your mobile app we can continue the development and further improve the product, pause the development for you to gather feedback from your clients or switch to support with an SLA agreement.
